About Us

Hello, our names are Brandon and Skye.

We are the co-founders of Increase My Love. We thought it would be fitting to include a photo so you can get to know us better.

This photo was taken in Cannon Beach, Oregon, where we got married back in 2005.

We live in Port Townsend, Washington, a small Victorian Seaport on the beautiful Olympic Peninsula of western Washington.

We love people, animals, outdoors and nature, travel, fun and life.

Increase My Love Backstory

If you are wondering why we created Increase My Love, here is our backstory.

A very good friend of ours extended unconditional love to us at a time when we needed it most. We noticed how much better we felt and we were hooked.

That single expression of love helped us realize that it was possible to have more love, joy and peace. From then on we have both actively cultivated more unconditional love in our lives.

Feeling the positive changes firsthand, we wanted to help friends and family feel better as well. After sharing love with them, they would frequently report how much better they felt.

Eventually we decided that we should find a way to help more people experience more unconditional love.

Increase My Love was created from a single act of unconditional love. We feel like this is one of the best qualities of love because it can have a very large impact on the world.

After years of developing and testing, we are now ready to invite people to Increase My Love.
